PHASE 00 — DO ONCE
Build Your Foundation

Before you film anything, lock in the decisions that make daily production fast and consistent.

01
Choose a Niche with Evergreen Demand
Pick one niche and stick to it. The algorithm rewards consistency. Choose something with high search volume, low production complexity, and content you can generate daily.
Finance Tips
High CPM, endless topics
History / Facts

Motivational
Simple visuals, broad audience
AI / Tech Tips
Trending, screen-recordable
Health & Wellness
Infographic-friendly, evergreen
True Crime / Mystery
High watch time, viral potential
02
Set Up Your Asset Library
Create a folder system you'll reuse daily: /Scripts, /Voiceovers, /Footage, /Music, /Exports. Bookmark 3–5 stock footage sites and pre-download a batch of royalty-free background music. Having assets ready eliminates the #1 daily bottleneck.
Pexels (free) Pixabay (free) Coverr (free) Storyblocks (paid) YouTube Audio Library
03
Create a Brand Template
Design one video template: same font, color palette, lower-thirds style, and intro/outro (3–5 sec each). Save it as a project file in your editor. Every daily video starts from this template — you never design from scratch again.
CapCut (free) Adobe Premiere DaVinci Resolve (free) OpusClip

PHASE 02 — DAILY · ~5 MIN
Voiceover Production

Your voice never appears. AI voices are now indistinguishable from human narrators at good settings.

01
Generate AI Voiceover
Paste your script into a text-to-speech tool. Choose a voice that matches your brand (warm, authoritative, energetic). ElevenLabs produces the most natural results. Download as MP3. Most platforms give enough free credits for 1–2 videos/day.
ElevenLabs (free tier) Murf.ai (free tier) PlayHT (free tier) TikTok TTS (built-in)
02
Optional: Record Your Own Voice (Stay Faceless)
If you want authenticity without showing your face, record yourself reading the script. Use your phone's voice memo app in a quiet room. Run it through Adobe Podcast Enhance (free) for instant studio-quality cleanup. This takes 5 minutes and keeps the channel 100% faceless.
Adobe Podcast Enhance (free) Audacity (free)
PHASE 03 — DAILY · ~20–40 MIN
Video Assembly

The most time-intensive phase. Keep it fast by working from your template and using AI-assisted tools.

01
Open Your Template & Import Voiceover
Open your saved editor project template. Import your voiceover file. This is your audio backbone — all visuals will be cut to match it. Never start with visuals first.
02
Add Stock Footage or Screen Recordings
Pull clips from your pre-downloaded asset library. Match visuals to what's being said — every 3–5 seconds, cut to a new clip to maintain attention. For finance/info topics, use simple animated text over B-roll. For tech topics, screen record your workflow.
03
Add Captions Automatically
Auto-generate captions from your voiceover. Captions increase watch time by 12–30% across all platforms. Use bold, high-contrast text. CapCut's auto-caption feature handles this in one click.
CapCut Auto-Caption (free) Submagic (paid) Clideo (free)
04
Add Background Music & Sound Effects
Pull a track from your pre-saved music folder. Set volume to 8–15% of voiceover level — it should be felt, not heard. Add 1–2 sound effects at key moments (whoosh on text reveal, chime on CTA). Export at 1080×1920 for Shorts/Reels/TikTok.
PHASE 04 — DAILY · ~10 MIN
Publish & Distribute

One video, multiple platforms. Maximize reach without making multiple videos.

01
Write an Optimized Title & Description with AI
Prompt: "Write 3 YouTube title options for a video about [TOPIC]. Make them curiosity-driven, under 60 characters, and include the keyword. Then write a 150-word SEO description." Pick the best title. Paste the description in.
02
Create a Thumbnail (60 Seconds)
Open a saved Canva thumbnail template. Change the text and background image only. Bold text + high contrast + a number or question mark = high CTR. Export and upload. Never design from scratch.
Canva (free)
03
Cross-Post to All Platforms
Upload the same 9:16 video to YouTube Shorts, TikTok, Instagram Reels, and Facebook Reels. Use a scheduler to queue everything in one session. You only create once — the algorithm does the rest across four audiences.
Buffer (free tier) Metricool Later

The Golden Rule of Daily Posting

Speed beats perfection. A good video published daily will always outperform a perfect video published weekly. The algorithm rewards consistency and volume. Your first 30 videos are practice — ship them anyway. Optimize based on what the data tells you after week 4.
